Dog rescue center

As the quality of life improves, people pay more and more attention to animals. Dogs are a particularly good animal, and many people like to keep dogs. However, there are still many dogs that are lost, or there is no nearby hospital to treat them. The design of our center is to better serve the dog-loving community. Here you can adopt a dog, or upload information about lost dogs. There is also information about nearby hospitals that can treat dogs.
